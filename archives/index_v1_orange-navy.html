African Continental Free Trade Area Shows Promising Growth in Q2 2023

Our analysis of reports from BBC Africa, France 24, and RFI reveals a 15% increase in intra-African trade since AfCFTA implementation. African Diaspora Investments Reach Record High in 2023

RFI and France 24 report remittances and direct investments from Africans abroad surpass $100 billion. Technology (35%) and real estate (28%) lead sectors, with Nigeria, Ghana, and Kenya receiving 60% of total diaspora capital flows.
